How SuiFlow Works

Sending SUI is as simple as dialing a number.

1. Dial the USSD Code

Start by dialing *384*2005# on any mobile phone. This works even on basic feature phones (kabambes) with no internet connection. The USSD menu will guide you through creating your secure SuiFlow wallet and setting up your unique PIN for transactions.

USSD Terminal

$ *384*2005#

Connecting...

2. Send SUI Instantly

Once in the menu, select "Send SUI". You'll be prompted to enter the recipient's phone number and the amount you wish to send. Confirm the transaction with your secure PIN. Powered by the Sui Blockchain, your SUI is transferred with sub-second finality and incredibly low fees, reaching the recipient almost instantly.

3. Cash Out to Local Currency

The recipient receives an SMS notification and can then access their SUI through the same USSD code. From their SuiFlow wallet, they can choose to cash out their SUI directly to their local mobile money account or bank, depending on regional integrations. It's a seamless transition from crypto to fiat, empowering financial inclusion.
